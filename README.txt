How to update your Netlify site

1. Download this ZIP.
2. Extract it.
3. Confirm you see:
   - index.html
   - style.css
   - script.js
   - assets/profile.jpg
4. Drag and drop the extracted folder into your Netlify site's Production deploys area.
5. Refresh the live site.

Note:
- The Resume buttons point to assets/Raghul_Resume.pdf.
- Add your real resume PDF there later if you want the download to work.
